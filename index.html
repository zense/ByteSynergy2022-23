About ByteSynergy


ByteSynergy, is a 54 hour long hackathon conducted by the students of IIIT Bangalore as a part of Synergy - The Techfest of IIITB. It is a chance to put your creative minds against some of the best student developers in our country.


Hack away and bring your ideas to fruition, using any techstack, language, or open source codebase! Make apps that will not only contribute to the world of open source development, but also help people improve their quality of life.

Tracks

Task Automation

The goal here is to take a task that would generally require a lot of human intervention to be performed and try to automate. The automation can be something as simple as reading a user’s information and filling a form for them or creating a complex chatbot that can adapt, to better help the user. As long as your idea has the potential to have an impact on the system, it’s good to go!

Social Issues

The premise here is simple, take any problem that you see around you in your everyday life and try to implement clever solutions for it! Whether it is related to solving theft issues in societies, or finding parking spaces in crowded places or improving attendance systems in schools.

Open Innovity

It is time to get your brain juices flowing! This track is one where you can express the full extent of your creativity. Anything from game-dev to an android or web app, Augmented Reality or any other field that interests you. There are no limits to your creativity and we look forward to seeing your extraordinary creations!

Healthcare

It is now your turn to contribute to the fast paced world of medical technology! Be it making applications that are able to better collect and organise information to help medical professionals, or applications to help patients in selecting the doctors that will best be able to treat them based on their symptoms or an AI/ML based solution for problems plaguing the medical world. The possibilities are endless and we look forward to seeing what you guys come up with!

All students currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate degree program are welcome to participate in the hackathon.
